Starfield Resources


About

Welcome to the starfield resources fan wiki. Here you will find information on some of the coolest planets in the game, alongside the best spaceships and most unique characters that you'll meet as you explore the universe.

The goal for this resource hub is to act as an encyclopedia almost, without being a resource lore dump at the same time.

This resource hub mainly highlights the subjective nature of the best planets, ships, and characters in the game.

At some point in time once the development team adds in land vehicles and land maps, those sections may get added to this resource hub as more updates in general come to the game.

For now, enjoy this confined but also subjective list of some of the coolest planets, ships and characters that you'll have the chance to encounter while exporing the Starfield universe.

I have traveled through Starfield's cosmos and have come across five planets that have had a lasting impact on my experiences:

  3. Horizon's Edge
    • A desert region with mysterious ruins and golden sands waiting to be discovered.
  4. Aurora
    • A mysterious planet in a forest covered in its namesake phenomenon's captivating glow.
  5. Celestial Haven
    • A view of the vast space adorned with islands that float and swirling storms.

Even though these five planets are particularly notable, countless other worlds in the vast Starfield universe still need to be fully explored. As I continue my journey, I eagerly anticipate the wonders that await me, knowing that the Starfield universe holds endless adventures.

Characters

Out of all the unique characters in Starfield, these top 5 remian some of my favorite in the Starfield universe:

  • Vasco stands as an exemplar of technological innovation—a remarkable robotic companion meticulously engineered for the precise task of piloting starships through the vast expanse of the cosmos. His creation represents the pinnacle of technological achievement.
  • Vasco is unique due to his technical prowess alone. Beyond his function as a stellar navigator, Vasco embodies a unique blend of personality traits, thanks to the seamless integration of advanced AI.
  • Sam Coe is a brilliant engineer and indispensable member of our crew, adept at crafting and repairing the intricate machinery that keeps our starship running smoothly.
  • Mr. Coe is unqiue due to his unmatched expertise in technology and his unwavering dedication to ensuring our ship's functionality, even in the face of the most daunting challenges. His resourcefulness and ingenuity inspire confidence and admiration among the crew, making him a cornerstone of our success in the vast expanse of space.
  • Jessamine Griffin is a Crimson Fleet member and a passionate biologist, driven by her insatiable curiosity to unravel the mysteries of alien life forms and their ecosystems.
  • Jessamine is unique due to her unparalleled medical expertise, and her relentless pursuit of scientific discovery. Her compassionate nature and keen intellect not only save lives but also enrich our understanding of the cosmos, making her an invaluable member of our crew and a beacon of hope in the darkest reaches of space.
  • Sarah Morgan is a main member of Constellation and a seasoned diplomat with a knack for navigating the delicate intricacies of interstellar politics and forging alliances between disparate factions.
  • Sarah is unique due to her impeccable negotiation skills, her ability to defuse tensions with grace and tact, and her unwavering commitment to promoting peace and cooperation throughout the galaxy. Her charismatic charm and strategic acumen make her an indispensable asset.
  • Mathis Castillo is a seasoned warrior with a mysterious past, renowned for his unparalleled combat skills and unwavering loyalty to his comrades and people of Akila.
  • Mathis's coolness lies in his stoic demeanor, his unwavering courage in the face of danger, and his unshakeable commitment to protecting those under his care. His enigmatic aura and formidable presence command respect and admiration among the crew, instilling confidence and trust in his leadership as we navigate space.
